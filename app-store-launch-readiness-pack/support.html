Support

For questions about the App Store Launch Readiness Pack, contact the seller through the marketplace where you purchased the product or use the support email configured on the product listing.

What support covers

  • Broken files or unclear instructions in the pack.
  • Questions about using the local Markdown templates or offline generator.
  • Correction updates for product materials.

What support does not cover

  • App Store Connect operation, upload, signing, or review submission.
  • Legal, tax, banking, or App Review guarantees.
  • Custom review of private apps unless sold as a separate fixed-scope service.
