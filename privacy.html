Revenue Pulse Studio Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-13

Revenue Pulse Studio is a local-first utility for reviewing user-owned App Store sales exports.

Data Collection

Revenue Pulse Studio does not collect personal data, does not track users, and does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Local Files And Sales Exports

The app lets you paste or import CSV/TSV sales export text that you own. Processing happens on device. The app does not save full report text automatically after generation. If you tap Save Locally, the current report text is stored under the Revenue Pulse Studio storage namespace so you can reopen the app and continue the same workflow. You can remove stored report text from the app's Clear Local Report action.

Network Use

The current release does not require an account, does not use a self-hosted server, and does not connect to App Store Connect. Exported Markdown files are saved only where you choose through the iOS file exporter.

Purchases

The Markdown export feature is unlocked with a $9.99 one-time non-consumable in-app purchase handled by Apple StoreKit. Revenue Pulse Studio does not receive your payment card details.

Data Sharing

Revenue Pulse Studio does not sell, share, or transmit your imported sales export data.
